My School

Rotary Public School, located in Hunsur, Mysore district, is a highly esteemed educational institution renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and impactful social service initiatives. With a prominent presence in the heart of Hunsur, the school has established itself as a beacon of quality education and community engagement. Rotary Public School's reputation extends beyond its academic achievements, as it actively participates in various social service programs that benefit the local community. Through initiatives like clean-up drives, educational outreach, and healthcare camps, the school exemplifies its dedication to making a positive difference in society. The school's emphasis on holistic education is evident in its comprehensive curriculum, designed to nurture both intellectual and ethical growth among students. Experienced educators provide a nurturing environment where students can develop critical thinking skills, creativity, and a strong sense of social responsibility. Rotary Public School's legacy of academic excellence and active community involvement makes it a respected institution not only in Hunsur but also in the wider educational landscape. Its unique blend of academic rigor and social consciousness prepares students to be not just successful professionals, but also responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.
