Glimpse Of My HomeTown

Hunsur is a town located in the Mysore district of Karnataka, India. It is known for its scenic beauty, with lush green landscapes and wildlife reserves nearby. Hunsur is also famous for its cultural heritage, with several temples and historical sites to explore. It's a great place to visit if you're interested in experiencing the rich history and natural beauty of Karnataka. 🌳🏞️
